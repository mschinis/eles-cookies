# Features Roadmap

## High Impact / Quick Wins

### 1. Order Tracking Page
A `/order/[id]` page showing current status: **Confirmed → Baking → Out for delivery → Fulfilled**.
Reduces post-purchase anxiety and feels premium without requiring a full logistics integration.

### 2. SMS / WhatsApp Notifications
Send a message when the order is confirmed, and another when it's fulfilled.
Cyprus customers expect WhatsApp; this personal touch differentiates from larger retailers.

### 3. Delivery Date Selection at Checkout
Let customers pick a preferred delivery date at checkout.
Helps with baking batch planning and reduces failed deliveries — especially important for gift orders.

---

## Revenue Drivers

### 4. Custom Flavour Mix Notes
Free-text field at checkout: *"Any special requests?"*
Low effort to implement, high perceived value, and differentiates from competitors.

### 5. Recurring Orders / Subscription
A "remind me in 4 weeks" email opt-in, or a full monthly subscription option.
Drives repeat purchases without requiring customers to remember to reorder.

### 6. Gift Cards
Digital gift cards with zero fulfilment cost.
High-margin product particularly powerful around holidays (Christmas, Mother's Day, Easter).

### 7. Upsell Nudge at Checkout
If a customer selects 12 cookies, show an inline prompt: *"Add 12 more for 10% off."*
The discount logic already exists — this just surfaces it at the right moment to increase average order value.

---

## Trust & Conversion

### 8. Reviews / Testimonials on Menu Cards
A single authentic customer quote per flavour on the menu cards (e.g. *"My kids demolished these in 10 minutes"*).
Increases confidence for first-time buyers at the point of decision.

### 9. Stock / Availability Indicator
Show limited availability messaging, e.g. *"Only taking 3 more orders this week."*
Scarcity is a proven conversion driver for small-batch food businesses.

### 10. FAQ Page
Cover allergens, shelf life, delivery radius, and gift wrapping details.
Reduces pre-purchase hesitation and cuts down on support messages to the owner.

---

## Operational

### 11. Weekly Order Summary Email to Owner
Automated Monday morning email: total orders, revenue, and most popular flavours for the past week.
Helps with ingredient planning without requiring a Payload login.

### 12. Bulk Fulfilment Workflow in Admin
A "Mark all confirmed orders as fulfilled" bulk action in Payload that auto-sends a *"your order is on its way"* email.
Currently fulfilment status updates and customer notifications are fully manual.

---

## Priority

If prioritising, these three have the most direct impact on the customer journey and revenue:

1. **Order tracking page** — closes the post-purchase experience gap
2. **Delivery date selection** — reduces failed deliveries and helps with planning
3. **Upsell nudge** — converts 12-cookie orders into 24-cookie orders using existing discount logic
